About Me

I was born is Los Angeles California in 1982, where i currently reside. I'm of Cuban descent, my parents migrated here in 1980 from La Habana Cuba. My mother left when i was a baby and my father past away when i was a teenager. Life has definately given me some difficult barriers to overcome. Fortunately for me i was able to find balance... I love art! I'm able to draw with ball point pens. I can draw anything from Portraits to cars and anything inbetween, i am also a certified tattoo Artist. I make car decals, custom shirts, i'm very hands on. I build my own shelves, i install my own car dvr systems, and i seldomly buy something pre-built. The most important detail that you can know about me is that i like to do things that are challenging.
